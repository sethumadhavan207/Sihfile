What is DBT?

DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) ensures that scholarship funds reach students directly in their bank accounts, avoiding delays and leakages. This helps students receive their benefits transparently and quickly.

Benefits of DBT

• Direct transfer to bank accounts
• Transparency and accountability
• No middlemen involved
• Faster processing of scholarships
